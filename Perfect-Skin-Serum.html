Description

This serum is a unique blend of essential botanical oils and herbs designed to provide improvement on the appearance of acne scars, blemishes and dark spots. Many of the ingredients contain natural anti-bacterial properties, which will help to keep pores clean and clear, preventing break-outs and helping to regenerate the skin cells for brighter, clearer skin.
Our products are created with only natural ingredients; free of any chemicals and synthetic ingredients that are harmful and damaging to your skin.

Ingredients

Tea tree oil, Tamanu oil, neem oil, moringa oil, emu oil, Eucalyptus Extracts, Lavender Flower Extracts, jojoba oil, lemon extracts, geranium essential oil and Vitamin E.

How to use

Massage 3 drops onto a freshly washed and slightly damp face, twice daily for beautiful and healthy skin.

Store in a cool and dry place.
