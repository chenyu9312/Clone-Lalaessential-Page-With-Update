Description

Our products are created with only natural ingredients; free of any chemicals and synthetic ingredients that are harmful and damaging to your skin.

This face mask instantly cleanses, removes impurities, absorbs excess oil, soothes and refreshes the skin. Perfect for oily and acne prone skin.

Ingredients

Organic Tea Tree oil, Aloe Vera Juice, Kaolin Clay, Bentonite Clay, French Green Clay, Rhassoul Clay, Emulsifying Wax, Xantham Gum, Stearic Acid, Vegetable Glycerin, Coconut Oil, Sweet Almond Oil, Rose Hips Extract.

How to use

Smooth a thin layer onto cleansed skin. Leave on for 30 minutes. Then rinse off with clean water and pat skin dry. Use once or twice per week, after cleansing.

Store in a cool and dry place.
